CTiS: Impact of Technology Site

The internet has become a part of life, but it’s also a double-edged sword. ​

- What makes the internet's effect on politics important?​

- E-democracy.​

- Large scale many-to-many communication. Meme warfare.​

- The butterfly and domino effects with easily shared information.​

- Fake news, filter bubbles, echo chambers.​

- Government censorship (internet content moderation).​

- Data collection for tailored candidate promotion.​

- Citizen mobilization – Donald Trump's Twitter messages. Woman’s march.​

Investigation:

We are going to investigate how the internet can be used by politicians to manipulate people and sway the vote count in their favor.

Either by using the internet to spark enthusiasm for themselves or bring other politicians down.

With our main goal being to see how big of and impact the internet has on politics.
